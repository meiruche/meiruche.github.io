Meiru Che's Biography

I am an Adjunct Professor in the Department of Computer Science at Concordia University Texas. Before summer 2019, I worked as an Assistant Professor at Concordia University Texas. My research interests include software architecture and design, software verification and testing, software evolution, and program analysis.

I received my Ph.D. from The University of Texas at Austin in 2014, my M.E. in Computer Software and Theory from Institute of Software Chinese Academy of Sciences, and my B.S. in Computer Science from Jilin University.
