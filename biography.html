Meiru Che's Biography

Meiru Che received the PhD degree in Software Engineering from the University of Texas at Austin, USA, 2014. Her research interests lie in software engineering, mobile software systems, and artificial intelligence. She is a Research Scientist with Data61, Commonwealth Scientific and Industrial Research Organization (CSIRO), Australia, from 2022 to 2023, and was an Assistant Professor from 2017 to 2021 with the Department of Computer Science at Concordia University Texas, USA. Before that, she was a Lecturer in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of Texas at Austin from 2015 to 2017.

Meiru Che received her M.E. in Computer Software and Theory from Institute of Software Chinese Academy of Sciences, and my B.S. in Computer Science from Jilin University.
