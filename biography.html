Meiru Che's Biography

I am an Adjunct Professor in the Department of Computer Science at Concordia University Texas. Before summer 2019, I worked as an Assistant Professor at Concordia University Texas. My research focuses on software engineering, artificial intelligence, specifically on software testing and analysis, software architecture and design, and software evolution. I have recently started working on applying AI in software engineering.

I received my Ph.D. from The University of Texas at Austin in 2014, my M.E. in Computer Software and Theory from Institute of Software Chinese Academy of Sciences, and my B.S. in Computer Science from Jilin University.
